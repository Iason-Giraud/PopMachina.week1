# Iason's Web Page ![Image of Pop-machina](https://d1rkab7tlqy5f1.cloudfront.net/BK/Onderzoek/Onderzoeksthemas/Circular_Built_Environment/Projects/Pop_Machina_Logo.PNG "PoPMachina") ### What is Pop Machina? =============================================================== - Pop-Machina is a Horizon 2020 project that seeks to highlight and reinforce the links between the maker movement and circular economy in order to promote environmental sustainability and generate socio-economic benefits in European cities. --- ### What do you expect from Pop Machina Academy? ================================================================     Pop Machina academy, will be a overview of the circular economy movement and a crash course in all necasairy skills that a maker champion needs to hone down in order to support his local fablab or maker space. The idea of a fablab that specializes in circular economy, rests on serendipity, meaning finding resourceful ways of taking advantage of local resource chains, identifying ways to transform waste from one production process into input of another.     In that way the already established paradigm of contemporary production PITO (Products in- Trash out) will gradually transcend into DIDO (data in- Data out)      Cities in the circular economy network: ![Image of cities in network](https://www.openaccessgovernment.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/citypassport_map_vector.jpg) --- Skills expected to be persued during the academy: \- Digital Design \- Digital Fabrication \- Circular way of thinking \- Web Development \- PCB prototyping \- Arduino Basics
